I am a postdoctoral scholar in the Department of Mechanical Engineering at the University of California Berkeley. My research interests span robotics, astrodynamics, autonomy, control theory, machine learning, game theory and optimization among others. The broad question that I am exploring is: How to guarantee the resilience of autonomous systems?
