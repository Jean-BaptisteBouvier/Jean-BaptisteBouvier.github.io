I am a postdoctoral researcher in the Department of Aerospace Engineering at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign. My research interests span robotics, astrodynamics, autonomy, control theory, machine learning, game theory and optimization among others. The broad question that I am exploring is: How to guarantee the resilience of autonomous systems?
